Payroll Services in Stirling

Outsourced payroll services for Stirling small businesses

We provide payroll services for small businesses in Stirling, Bridge of Allan, Dunblane and across Central Scotland. Whether you pay staff weekly or monthly, we take care of PAYE, pension auto-enrolment and HMRC submissions so you don’t have to.

Stirling’s economy — from tourism and hospitality to professional services and retail — means many local employers need reliable, compliant payroll without the cost of in-house staff or complex software. We work with Stirling-based SMEs and family businesses who want payroll and small business HR support that’s accurate, on time and easy to understand.

Getting payroll wrong — even once — can mean penalties from HMRC, damaged employee trust, and hours of your time untangling the mess. Our outsourced payroll service removes that risk entirely. You get a dedicated contact who knows your business, processes your payroll on schedule, and stays on top of every legislative change so you don’t have to.

What’s included in our payroll service

  • Weekly or monthly payroll processing
  • PAYE and income tax calculations
  • Pension auto-enrolment administration and contributions
  • Real Time Information (RTI) submissions to HMRC
  • P45, P60, P11D and year-end forms
  • Digital payslips and payroll reports
  • National Minimum Wage and National Living Wage compliance
  • Statutory pay calculations (SSP, SMP, SPP)
  • New starter and leaver processing

Why Stirling businesses choose outsourced payroll

Many small businesses in Stirling start out processing payroll themselves — until one month something goes wrong. A late RTI submission, a miscalculated pension contribution, a missed National Living Wage increase. The penalties and admin time quickly outweigh any saving from doing it in-house.

Outsourcing to Crawford Consultancy means your payroll is handled by someone whose whole job is getting it right. We stay current with HMRC requirements, the latest National Living Wage rates, and pension compliance obligations, so you don’t need to. And because we also provide HR support, your payroll and people management work together — not in separate silos.

A local alternative to national payroll bureaus

Large payroll bureaus cover thousands of clients across the UK. With Crawford Consultancy, you get direct access to a real person who knows your business by name. We’re based in Falkirk and serve businesses across Stirling, the Forth Valley and Central Scotland. If something needs sorting quickly, you speak to us — not a call centre.

We regularly work with businesses in Stirling city centre, Bridge of Allan, Dunblane, and across the wider Stirling Council area, as well as businesses in Alloa and the Clackmannanshire area.
