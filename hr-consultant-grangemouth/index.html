Payroll services in Grangemouth

We run weekly and monthly payroll for Grangemouth businesses, handling PAYE calculations, pension auto-enrolment, Real Time Information (RTI) submissions to HMRC, payslips, P45s, P60s and year-end processing. We take the compliance burden off your desk entirely.

Getting HR and payroll from one provider means your employment contracts, pay records and compliance obligations stay consistent. Changes in HR — new starters, leavers, pay rises, role changes — are immediately reflected in payroll. Nothing slips through the cracks.
