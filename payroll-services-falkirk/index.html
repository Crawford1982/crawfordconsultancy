Payroll Services in Falkirk

Outsourced payroll services for Falkirk small businesses

We handle payroll services for small businesses in Falkirk and across Central Scotland. Whether you pay staff weekly or monthly, we take care of PAYE, pension auto-enrolment, and HMRC submissions so you don't have to.

Accurate, on time, every time. No need for an in-house payroll team or expensive software — we do it for you. Crawford Consultancy is based in Falkirk, which means you get a genuinely local service from someone who understands the Central Scotland business community.

Payroll errors are costly. A missed RTI submission, an incorrect PAYE calculation or a late pension contribution can result in HMRC fines and unhappy employees. Our payroll service removes that risk — we stay on top of every regulatory change so you can focus on running your business.

What's included in our Falkirk payroll service

  • Weekly or monthly payroll processing
  • PAYE and income tax calculations
  • Pension auto-enrolment administration and contributions
  • Real Time Information (RTI) submissions to HMRC
  • P45, P60 and year-end processing
  • Digital payslips and payroll reports
  • Statutory pay (SSP, SMP, SPP) calculations
  • National Minimum Wage and National Living Wage compliance
  • New starters and leavers managed

Payroll and HR — together under one roof

Many Falkirk businesses use separate providers for HR and payroll. This creates gaps — employment contract changes that aren't reflected in payroll, absence records that don't sync, compliance obligations that fall between the cracks. Crawford Consultancy provides both payroll and HR support together, so everything stays consistent and nothing gets missed.

We serve businesses throughout the Falkirk Council area including Grangemouth, Larbert, Stenhousemuir, Bo'ness, Denny, Bonnybridge and beyond. We also work with businesses in Stirling and across Central Scotland, and support remote clients throughout the UK.
