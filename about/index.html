Who We Are

Local support that fits your business

Crawford Consultancy is a Falkirk-based consultancy specialising in HR, payroll, compliance and admin support for small businesses, sole traders and family-run companies across the Central Belt of Scotland.

We understand that small businesses need practical, jargon-free advice — not corporate templates or lengthy contracts. Whether you're taking on your first employee, need help with payroll and pensions, or want someone to keep your people and compliance in order, we're here to help.

We offer flexible support: ad-hoc when you need it, monthly retainers, or project-based work. No fixed packages, no long-term commitment required. Get in touch for a free, no-obligation conversation about what you need.
