Practical HR and payroll for small businesses

We provide outsourced HR and payroll support for small businesses across Falkirk, Stirling, Grangemouth and Central Scotland. Whether you employ one person or a growing team, we help you stay compliant, manage people properly, and keep payroll accurate and on time.

Many small business owners find themselves handling HR and payroll with little or no formal training, relying on internet searches and hoping they've got it right. Why outsource HR and payroll?

Hiring a full-time HR manager or payroll administrator is rarely practical for a small business. It's expensive, and for many businesses the workload doesn't justify a full-time hire. Outsourcing gives you the same quality of support — proper documentation, accurate payroll, solid employment law advice — at a fraction of the cost.

You also get continuity. There's no holiday cover to arrange, no sick days when your payroll needs to go out, and no knowledge walking out the door when someone leaves. We're your HR and payroll team, available when you need us.
